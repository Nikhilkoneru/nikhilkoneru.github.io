Careers

Come join us and change the way governments work.

Career

We believe that learning never stops. Whether in the office, on-site at our customers’ locations or outside of working hours, we’re dedicated to providing opportunities for employees to advance their careers. Every employee is eligible for tuition reimbursement for both educational courses and relevant certifications.

Community

The community is home to our employees, customers, family and friends and we’re thankful to be a part of a growing and vibrant region. We believe that it is important to demonstrate our gratitude, so we participate in a number of events that support local charities and enrich our community and offer our employees time off to volunteer.

Health

We care about the work-life balance and the health of every employee. Octo offers a generous paid time off benefit that escalates quickly going into the second year of employment. We also offer comprehensive health, vision and dental insurance benefits for all employees. When new members join your family, we offer paid maternity and paternity leave too!

FINANCIAL

Octo believes that hard work deserves fair compensation and we are proud to offer competitive salaries. We also share in our employees’ success by recognizing and rewarding exceptional performance through our corporate bonus programs.

SOCIAL

While the work we do for our government customers is serious business, we pride ourselves on creating a unique culture. We believe in the power of camaraderie and you will often find us at sporting events and concerts as well as our annual summer picnic and holiday party.
