About Us

The pace at which the government is changing, there is a need for technology consulting companies that rise to those challenges by taking a fresh approach to problems, solutions that get to market faster, offer service that exceed the customers’ expectations and disrupt the status quo.

Anika Systems is an outcome-driven technology consulting firm that helps federal agencies solve business problems and enable them for the future, with services and solutions spanning Data Management, Application Development, Enterprise Modernization, Cloud Solutions and Intelligent Automation Solutions.

We’re a team of thinkers, lifelong learners, makers, and doers that deeply understand the Federal government customers missions and goals. Our teams are deeply connected and bring their shared experiences and insights to each and every engagement.

Why choose us?

Anika Systems is an outcome-driven technology consulting company that assists Federal agencies in meeting their mission goals and prepare them for the future. We view our clients as partners and actively collaborate with them to achieve long-term success and make a significant contribution to their mission goals.

Engage

Deeply engaging with our government customers and making a real difference through long-term, outcome focused relationships ensures success that fulfils our people and fuels our growth.

Innovate

Our approach to Innovation involves identifying the best ideas and technologies, rapid prototyping (at our own cost) and delivering Minimum Viable Products and Solutions that solve real and tangible problems of our government customers.

Empower

By relentlessly focusing on our government customer’s mission, we empower them by delivering their goals successfully on time, on budget.
